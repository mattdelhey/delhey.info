Matthew J. Delhey

Matthew is a first-year PhD student in Philosophy at the University of Toronto. His research interests lie in continental philosophy and political theory (esp. Hegel and Marx), and their intersection with psychoanalysis, digital media studies, and feminism. Matthew's master’s thesis explored the issues of machine automation and the possibility of a post-work politics in Hegel’s social theory.

Matthew also freelances as a data scientist and has a technical background in statistics, data science, and machine learning.
