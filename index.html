Matthew J. Delhey

Matthew is a first-year PhD student in Philosophy at the University of Toronto. His research interests lie in continental philosophy, and political theory (esp. Hegel and Marx), and their intersection with psychoanalysis, digital media studies, and queer theory. Matthew's master’s thesis explored the issue of machine automation and the possibility of a post-work politics in Hegel’s Philosophy of Right.

Prior to graduate school, Matthew worked as a data scientist and has a technical background in mathematical statistics, data science, and machine learning.
