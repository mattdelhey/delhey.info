I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Toronto, where I am completing a dissertation on Hegel's theory of institutions.

My research focuses on German idealism (especially Hegel), social and political philosophy, and critical social theory. Systematically, I am interested in institutional theories, theories of recognition, and critical theories of race, gender, labor, and technology. Historically, my work centers on the social philosophy of Hobbes, Fichte, Hegel, and Marx.

Previously, I worked in the technology sector as a data scientist.
