Matthew J. Delhey

Matthew is a first-year PhD student in Philosophy at the University of Toronto. His research interests lie primarily in continental philosophy and political theory, especially in the works of Hegel and Marx. He holds additional interests in psychoanalysis and digital media studies. Matthew's master’s thesis explored the issues of machine automation and the possibility of a post-work politics in Hegel’s social theory.
